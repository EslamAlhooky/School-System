School vision:
• A sentence that describes the hoped-for situation that a group seeks to reach.
It expresses an attractive, realistic and honest future for the school.
It also constitutes a challenge for the school community to reach it.
• The effective clear vision plays the role of a change agent to shorten the distance
between the "real situation" and the "hoped situation".
• A future dream in which all members of the school must participate in formulating it
and committing to the values​ and goals it contains,
and turning it into a reality embodied on the ground.
